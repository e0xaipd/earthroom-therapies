Therapies

Thoughtful care, tailored to you

Whether you’re navigating anxiety, trauma, grief, or life transitions, we’ll build a plan that fits your needs.

Individual Therapy

Weekly or bi‑weekly sessions focused on goals we set together, using modalities such as CBT, ACT, and somatic skills.

Couples & Relationships

Strengthen communication, repair trust, and deepen connection through structured, supportive conversations.

Trauma‑Informed Care

Gentle, titrated work that prioritizes stability and choice, incorporating grounding, EMDR‑informed techniques, and mindfulness.

Groups & Circles

Short, topic‑focused groups that encourage shared learning and practice in a safe, facilitated setting.

Mindfulness & Integration

Develop practical tools to notice patterns, respond skillfully, and integrate insights into daily life.

Earthroom Therapies

Whole-person therapy grounded in compassion, science, and nature.
